Housecat!

Housecat! is an indie jazz-rock band based in North Carolina, blending moody melodies with hard-hitting, hip-hop-inspired grooves. Fronted by singer-songwriter Jake Hodges, the band formed in 2024 and has since built a reputation for energetic live shows across Boone, Charlotte, and intimate house venues. With a growing catalog of singles available on all streaming platforms, Housecat! continues to craft a unique sound that bridges jazz, rock, and modern rhythms.
